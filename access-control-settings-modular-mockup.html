Check-in & Access NEW

Decide who can enter — gate by a confirmed booking or an active plan. Turn on the check-in methods you need, connect door hardware, and set the rules for each.

QR check-in

How staff verify and let clients in by scanning their QR code at the door.
Turn on QR check-in
Adds a QR scanner to the Wix Owner app for staff, and a personal QR generator in the member app so each client has a code to scan at the door.
Include QR in booking confirmation email
Send the QR in the confirmation email too, so clients can check in without installing the member app. Turn on QR check-in to enable.
Check-in window
How early and how late clients can check in around the session start time. Turn on QR check-in to enable.
15 min before → 10 min afterEdit
Let members enter with an active plan
Members with a connected plan can check in without a booking. Connect the plans that grant entry. Turn on QR check-in to enable.

Intake forms

Collect waivers and consent forms from clients. A client’s intake form status appears when they check in.
📝
Intake forms
Build and manage the forms clients fill in. Their signed/expired/missing status shows on the check-in screen so staff can act at the door.

Kiosk check-in

Let clients check themselves in at a self-service tablet at your entrance.
📱
Check-In app
Install the Check-In app on a tablet at your entrance for self-service check-in — clients scan their QR or find their name. This is a separate external app.

Door access

Unlock a physical door automatically for eligible members, by integrating an external provider like Kisi.

Sign in to your Kisi account and allow Wix to read your doors and send unlock signals. You’ll pick which doors to map next.

Choose which Kisi doors the check-in gate should control. You can change this later under Manage doors.
